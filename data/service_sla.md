# Service Level Agreement (SLA) & Support Tiers

This SLA defines our system uptime guarantees, downtime credits, and customer support response times.

## 1. Uptime Commitment

Adsparkx commits to maintaining a monthly system uptime of at least **99.9%** (excluding planned maintenance windows which are announced 48 hours in advance).

### SLA Credits
If monthly uptime drops below our commitment, you are eligible for subscription credits:
*   **Uptime 99.0% to 99.89%:** 10% credit of your monthly billing charge.
*   **Uptime 95.0% to 98.99%:** 25% credit of your monthly billing charge.
*   **Uptime below 95.0%:** 50% credit of your monthly billing charge.

Credits must be requested in writing by the account administrator within 30 days of the end of the billing month.

---

## 2. Customer Support Response Times

Support response times depend on the ticket severity and the customer's support tier:

*   **P1 (Critical - System Down):** Responded to within **1 hour** for Pro and Enterprise plans; 4 hours for Free plans.
*   **P2 (High - Core Feature Broken):** Responded to within **4 hours** for Pro and Enterprise; 12 hours for Free plans.
*   **P3 (Normal - General Questions):** Responded to within **24 hours** for all plans.
