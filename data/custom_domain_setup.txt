Adsparkx Custom Domain Configuration Guide

This article outlines how to link a custom domain to your Adsparkx Developer Portal (available on Pro and Enterprise plans).

1. Add Domain in Dashboard
1. Go to Settings -> Portal settings.
2. Under "Custom Domain", input your domain or subdomain (e.g., portal.yourdomain.com).
3. Copy the "CNAME Target Value" provided (usually: proxy.adsparkx.com).

2. Configure DNS Settings
Log in to your DNS provider (e.g., GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap) and create a CNAME record:
- Type: CNAME
- Host/Name: portal (or your chosen subdomain)
- Target/Value: proxy.adsparkx.com
- TTL: Automatic or 1 Hour

If you want to configure a root apex domain (e.g., yourdomain.com), create an ALIAS or ANAME record pointing to proxy.adsparkx.com.

3. SSL Verification & Provisioning
Once the DNS record is updated, our system automatically provisions a Let's Encrypt SSL certificate for your domain. 
Note: DNS propagation can take between 5 minutes and 24 hours. You can track propagation status in the dashboard. If SSL certificate fails to provision, check that no CAA DNS records are blocking Let's Encrypt.
