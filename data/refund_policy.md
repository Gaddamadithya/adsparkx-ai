# Adsparkx Refund Policy & Timeline

This document sets forth the refund conditions, eligibility, and processing timelines for all Adsparkx services.

## 1. Standard Refund Conditions

We want you to be completely satisfied with our platform. If you encounter any technical issues that make the platform unusable, you may request a refund under the following conditions:

*   **Monthly Subscriptions:** Monthly plans are eligible for a full refund if the request is submitted within **30 days** of the initial purchase or renewal date.
*   **Annual Subscriptions:** Annual plans are eligible for a full refund within **30 days** of the initial purchase. Renewals are eligible if requested within **14 days** of renewal.
*   **Usage Limitations:** To prevent abuse, refunds are only issued if your API usage during the billing cycle is under **5%** of your subscription tier's monthly limit.

---

## 2. Ineligibility for Refunds

Refunds will not be issued in the following scenarios:
*   Your account was suspended due to a violation of our Terms of Service (e.g., API spam, scraping, credential sharing).
*   You forgot to cancel your subscription before the renewal date and request a refund after the 30-day (for monthly) or 14-day (for annual) refund window.
*   Your usage exceeded the 5% threshold during the current billing cycle.

---

## 3. Processing Timeline & Refund Pathways

Once a refund request is approved:
*   Funds are processed back to the original payment method (Credit Card, PayPal, or Stripe).
*   **Timeline:** The refund will take **5 to 10 business days** to appear on your bank statement, depending on your financial institution.
*   You will receive a refund confirmation invoice email containing transaction reference codes.
