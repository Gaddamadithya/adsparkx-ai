Adsparkx Billing Policy and Cycle Guide

This document outlines our standard billing cycles, retry schedules, payment methods, and account grace periods.

1. Billing Cycle
Adsparkx subscriptions are billed automatically in advance on a recurring monthly or annual basis. 
Your billing date is established on the day you subscribe to a paid tier. For instance, if you upgrade to a Pro tier on the 14th of the month, your subsequent billing dates will fall on the 14th of each following month.

2. Failed Payments and Retry Schedule
If a charge fails due to card expiration, insufficient funds, or system issues, we execute a retry schedule:
- First Retry: 3 days after the initial payment failure.
- Second Retry: 6 days after the initial failure.
- Third Retry: 9 days after the initial failure.

During this period, your account will enter a "Billing Grace Period".

3. Grace Period
We offer a 14-day grace period during which your account remains active and features are not locked.
If the outstanding balance is not settled within 14 days, your account is automatically downgraded to the Free tier, and usage limits are restricted immediately.

4. Billing Invoices
Invoices are sent to the billing email address configured on your account. You can download PDF invoices from the dashboard: Settings -> Billing & Invoices -> Billing History.
