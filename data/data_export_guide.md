# Data Portability & Data Export Guide

This article provides steps for exporting your data from the Adsparkx platform for backups or migration.

## 1. Exporting Account Settings & Configurations

You can export all configuration profiles, route variables, and metadata in JSON format:
1. Navigate to the **Settings** menu.
2. Select the **Data & Portability** tab.
3. Click the **Export Configuration (JSON)** button.
4. A download will begin immediately containing a zipped archive of your JSON configs.

---

## 2. Exporting Database Records & Analytics

Analytics records can be exported in CSV format.
*   **Time Range:** You can select a date range (maximum 12 months per export).
*   **Method:** Click **Analytics** -> **Reports** -> **Export CSV**.
*   **Processing Time:** Small datasets export instantly. For files larger than 100MB, the system processes the request in the background and sends a download link to your email address within 30 minutes.

---

## 3. GDPR Requests & Account Deletion

Under GDPR regulations, users have the right to be forgotten.
*   To delete your account permanently, navigate to **Settings** -> **Account Management** -> **Delete Account**.
*   This action is irreversible. All data, billing records, and API logs will be permanently scrubbed after a **14-day hold period** (to prevent accidental deletions).
